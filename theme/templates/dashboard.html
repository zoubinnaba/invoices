Project Overview

The InvoiceApp is a comprehensive system designed to manage clients, products, product categories, and invoices efficiently. Below is a detailed breakdown of each module:

Clients

The Clients app allows you to store and manage your clients' information, including their names, addresses, contact details, and more. You can easily create client records.

Products

The Products app is responsible for managing all the products your company offers. Each product includes details like name, brand, reference, description, price, and applicable taxes (TVA).

Product Categories

Product Categories help you organize products into different categories for easier management and retrieval. You can create new categories or manage existing ones.

Invoices

The Invoices app is the core feature of the InvoiceApp. It allows you to create and manage invoices, assign them to clients, add products. You can view a list of invoices and export them as needed.
